The Problem: Manual Inventory Mayhem

Jewelry store staff were struggling with paper records and Excel sheets. Finding product info, tracking stock, and updating prices were time-consuming and prone to human error.

The Solution: A QR-Enabled Inventory Assistant

I designed a QR-based inventory system that lets admins add items, generate QR codes, attach them to products, and scan them via mobile for real-time checkout and details.
