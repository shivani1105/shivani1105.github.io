A URM Application

URM is a platform where URM candidates connect with academia, recruiters, and Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) officers for PhD studies, postdoc, and faculty positions, and help address the issue of underrepresentation in academia. It provides different dashboards to different roles. The roles are URM candidate, Academia, Recruiter, DEI Officer, Administrator. Using this platform, URM candidates can register and create profiles to showcase their qualifications and interests for the academics.
